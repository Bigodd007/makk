about-Us

Welcome to Ib Finest, where exceptional taste meets unmatched quality! Located at No. 15 Awolowo Avenue, New Estate, we have proud;y been serving our community since 2021. Over the past three years, we've become a destination for lovers of fine cuisine, offering a wide range of dishes that celebrate the best of Nigerian Flavors and international favorites.
From our signature Amala and Ewedu to sizzling grills, jollof and fried rice, moi-moi, pepper-soup, Barbeque, decadent desserts, every meal is crafted with premium ingredients at prices that suits every budget. Our mission is simple: to delight our customers with top-notch service, unbeatable quality, and a dining experience that lives up to our motto -"Where Every Bite Delivers Execellence!"
At Ib Finest, we're committed to becoming your first choice for delicious meals, whether you're dining in, taking out, or enjoying swift delivery to your doorstep. Thank you for letting us serve you!
